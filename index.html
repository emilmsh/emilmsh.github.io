Emil Halseth

Economist, Oslo Economics

I am an economist at Oslo Economics, where I work on competition economics and empirical market analysis. My research background is in industrial organization: how firms compete with each other in markets, with a particular focus on the grocery retail sector, using quasi-experimental methods and surveys.

Before joining Oslo Economics I was a PhD research scholar at the Department of Economics, NHH Norwegian School of Economics, and a PhD fellow at the Bergen Center for Competition Law and Economics.

Research

My work centres on empirical industrial organization and competition policy — market definition, merger analysis, and buyer power — with the Norwegian grocery sector as a recurring setting.
